About the Diner

Located in heart of Toronto, The 70's Diner is a bright and chill place to just hang out and have a great meal. We are open 24/7 for all those blazin' cool cats out there who like to stay out late. We serve Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner all day, everyday, because why not? Come for a coffee with the morning paper at midnight or have a milkshake and burger for breakfast. Get loose, get jiggy with it and enjoy yourself.
